The Pacific Northwest

The Pacific Northwest is a beatufiful area that includes Northern California, Oregon, Washington, and British Columbia. It is famous for its lush evergreen forests, rocky coastlines, flowing waterfalls, and abundant volcanoes. Some of the more notable cities in the PNW are Portland, OR; Seattle, WA; and Vancouver, BC. The PNW is an excellent place for hiking, kayaking, and sightseeing because of its seemingly never-ending natural beauty.
